    Aim of Solve it!

Many a times when in doubt,we go to some random websites which don't provide us a satisfactory explanation or just have give you information on an irrelevant topic..Even i have faced this problem many a times.


I always wanted a website where we get our answers based on how urgently we need the answer(cuz many of us have olympiads and stuff at diffferent times).I hope all the users and me myself will try our best together to respond to all your doubts within 24 hours.



Question of the week(Q.O.T.W)-(pronounced kote wee)


Q.O.T.W is an initiative by solve it....A tricky question will be shared every week and a google form will also be shared where the answers will be given.These Qs will be MCQs. Answer questions correctly and gain points!..The people are in the first three positions will be announced every month.


This according to Rudra,The owner of solve it will be an extremely fun and exciting part of this organisation as it not only be the will to get on the leaderboard but everyone will learn a question a week that means 52 in a year!
